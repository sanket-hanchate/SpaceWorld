Mercury

The Closest Planet to the Sun

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and the closest to the Sun. It experiences extreme temperatures and has a rocky, crater-filled surface.

Orbit Period: 88 Earth Days

Moons: None

Earth

Our Home, the Blue Planet

Earth is the only known planet that supports life. With vast oceans, a breathable atmosphere, and diverse ecosystems, it stands as a rare and beautiful world in the universe.

Diameter: 12,742 km

Orbit Period: 365 Days

Moons: 1 (The Moon)

Mars

The Red Planet

Mars is known for its red surface and has been a major focus for space exploration, hinting at the possibility of past life.

Diameter: 6,779 km

Orbit Period: 687 Days

Moons: 2

Saturn

The Ringed Planet

Diameter: 50,724 km

Orbit Period: 84 Years

Moons: 27

Neptune

Diameter: 49,244 km
