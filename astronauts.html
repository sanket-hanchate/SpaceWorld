Indian Space Explorers

Rakesh Sharma

The Pioneer

The first Indian to enter space in 1984 aboard the Soviet Soyuz T-11 mission. He spent nearly 8 days on the Salyut 7 station.

Kalpana Chawla

The Inspiration

Born in Karnal, she became the first woman of Indian origin in space. Her legacy continues to inspire millions of dreamers worldwide.

Sunita Williams

The Record-Setter

Known for her long-duration missions and record-breaking spacewalks, Sunita has logged over 320 days in space across multiple missions.

The Gaganyatris

The Future

Nair, Krishnan, Pratap, and Shukla. These IAF test pilots are currently training for India's historic maiden crewed mission.

Sirisha Bandla

The Researcher

In 2021, she flew on the Virgin Galactic Unity 22 mission, becoming the second India-born woman to go to space, where she conducted plant research.

Raja Chari

The Commander

An Indian-American test pilot who served as Commander of the NASA SpaceX Crew-3 mission to the ISS, spending 177 days in orbit.
